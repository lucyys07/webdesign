Welcome to your central hub for navigating college life in Alabama! Here you'll find a wide range of resources designed to support you through your academic journey, enhance your well-being, and prepare you for a successful career. Whether you're looking for financial aid information, academic support, or health services, we've got you covered.
Academic Support
Tutoring Services: Access tutoring across subjects like Math and Writing to enhance your grades; available through your university's tutoring center.
Study Tools: Utilize study guides, library resources, and research databases available through your university's library.
Workshops: Improve skills with workshops on study techniques and more, details provided by your university's events page.

Financial Aid and Scholarships
Scholarships: Find tailored scholarship opportunities for various student groups via your university's financial aid office.
Aid Counseling: Get personalized financial aid advice to manage your finances effectively at the financial aid office.
Budgeting Help: Use budgeting tools and tips for financial planning available through student financial services.

Health and Wellness
Mental Health: Access counseling and stress management at the university health center.
Recreational Sports: Participate in fitness programs and sports through the campus recreation department.
Nutrition: Get nutrition advice and healthy eating tips at the health center.
Career Planning
Career Services: Prepare for the job market with resume, interview, and networking resources offered by the career center.
Alumni Networks: Connect with alumni for guidance and job opportunities through the alumni association.
Professional Development: Join industry-related organizations through the career center for additional resources.
